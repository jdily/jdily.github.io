I-Chao Shen

ichao.shen@ui.is.s.u-tokyo.ac.jp

I am a project assistant professor (特任助教) at The University of Tokyo, working with Takeo Igarashi. My research focus on human-guided optimization, where I explore combinations between different human priors and numerical optimization and develop supportive system for visual content design.
